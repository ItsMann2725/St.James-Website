Benefits & Requisites

Chef de Partie - On-site - Culinary

Description

At St.James, we believe that culinary excellence is the heart of every unforgettable dining experience. As a Chef de Partie, you play a crucial role in our kitchen brigade, bringing your passion for gastronomy and precision to create dishes that delight our guests.

Benefits

  • Competitive hourly wage at $20.50 - $21/hour
  • Medical, vision, dental and life benefits available on the 1st month following full-time employment
  • Paid time off
  • 401(K) with a company match of up to 4% of deferred earnings
  • Employee Dining Discount, 20% to any item served at the time of order
  • UberEats discount available after 1 month of full-time employment

Responsibilities

  • Bring innovation to your section, creating dishes that showcase your culinary prowess while adhering to our standards of excellence.
  • Collaborate with fellow chefs, kitchen staff, and management to ensure smooth operations and the consistent delivery of exceptional dining experiences.
  • Maintain high standards of food quality, consistency, and hygiene, adhering to our recipes and presentation guidelines.
  • Foster a culture of continuous learning and growth, mentoring junior kitchen staff to hone their skills and contribute to the overall success of the team.

Requirements

  • Unprecedented work ethic
  • Proven experience as a Chef de Partie or a similar role in a reputable culinary establishment.
  • Thinks clearly, quickly, and maintains concentration during high pressure situations
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high-pressure kitchen environment.
  • Strong organizational and communication skills.
