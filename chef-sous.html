Benefits & Requisites

Sous Chef - On-site - Culinary

Description

As a Sous Chef at St.James, you will be the right hand to our Head Chef, responsible for overseeing daily kitchen operations, managing the brigade, and ensuring the delivery of top-notch dishes. Your culinary creativity, leadership skills, and attention to detail will be instrumental in maintaining our reputation for culinary excellence.

Benefits

  • Competitive hourly wage at $20.50 - $21/hour
  • Medical, vision, dental and life benefits available on the 1st month following full-time employment
  • Paid time off
  • 401(K) with a company match of up to 4% of deferred earnings
  • Employee Dining Discount, 20% to any item served at the time of order
  • UberEats discount available after 1 month of full-time employment

Responsibilities

  • Provide guidance and support to the kitchen team, fostering a positive and collaborative work environment. Assist in training and development, ensuring that each team member reaches their full potential.
  • Collaborate with the Head Chef to contribute to menu creation, bringing your culinary expertise to the table and ensuring that our offerings reflect innovation and quality.
  • Oversee day-to-day kitchen operations, including inventory management, quality control, and adherence to safety and hygiene standards.
  • Work closely with the Head Chef to translate culinary concepts into reality, ensuring the consistent delivery of exceptional dishes that exceed guest expectations.

Requirements

  • 1 or more years experience as a Sous Chef or in a similar leadership role within the culinary industry.
  • Strong leadership and communication skills.
  • Unprecedented work ethic
  • Ability to thrive in a fast-paced, high-pressure kitchen environment.
  • Passion for culinary arts and a commitment to maintaining the highest standards of quality.
